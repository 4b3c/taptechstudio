About us

What we do

TapTech Studio makes custom NFC-embedded 3D-printed keychains for your business or club. Each one is designed from your logo, 3D-printed, and programmed in-house, by two college students. We can create blank chips that let each user set them up individually, or preprogrammed chips that direct users to your preferred link.
